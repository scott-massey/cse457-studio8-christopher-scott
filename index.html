Analysis of Dog Breeds in NYC, by Borough

We gathered data about what kinds of dogs are owned where in NYC, and here's what we found.

By Christopher Patrick and Scott Massey


Dogs breeds by borough

Shown here are what dogs breeds people in different boroughs of NYC people own. The larger the circle, the more dogs of that breed are owned in that borough.

Dogs by age

Shown here are is the distribution of dogs in each borough by age. We found that people generally own younger dogs in Manhattan and Brooklyn, and older dogs in the other boroughs.

Most popular dog names

Shown here are the most popular dog names in NYC by borough. It looks like There are a lot of common names between the boroughs, such as Bella, Max, Rocky and Charlie.

Conclusions

Overall, the distributions between boroughs did not differ much between each other. It seems like smaller breeds, such as the Chihuahua are more popular. The most popular names are Bella, Max, Rocky, and Charlie. Age distributions also did not vary much.
